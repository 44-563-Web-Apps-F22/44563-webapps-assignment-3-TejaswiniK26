Dianne Duncan Thomas: Quilt ExpressionsThe warm comfort of handmade quilts was an integral part of Dianne Duncan Thomas childhood, so it did not seem unusual that she would take up the craft. She became the fifth generation, at least, in her family to find pleasure in the meticulous work. This creative longing was not surprising, given Thomas academic background in graphic design and art history at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln
